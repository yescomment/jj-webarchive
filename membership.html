The James Joyce Society:
2021 Membership

We're now all-digital in 2021! -- virtual meetings -- announcements by email -- and online payment for renewed/new memberships and guest attendance.

Annual dues: $25 (USD). Guest attendance: $7.

  1. To pay online:Use the pay by ZelleTM feature of the mobile application of your bank account. If necessary, go to your bank’s website to set up your account, the mobile application, and ZelleTM online payment. Send your payment to joycesocietydues@gmail.com.

Please note: The James Joyce Society is no longer accepting membership payments by mail.

1. ANNUAL MEMBERSHIP: Please pay $25 (USD) online via Zelle to joycesocietydues@gmail.com.

The Joyce Society asks for a voluntary $7 contribution for non-members attending our meetings.
